About Me

Hello! I'm Kallyan Chakma, a Research Fellow at the Bangladesh Forest Research Institute. I specialize in microbial genomics, metagenomics, and plant-microbe molecular interactions.

I completed my undergraduate studies in Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology from the University of Chittagong.

My current work focuses on developing fungal induction strategies for enhanced agarwood resin production, blending traditional knowledge with modern genomics tools.

Research Interests

  • 🧬 Microbial and comparative genomics
  • 🧫 Metagenomic analysis & antimicrobial resistance profiling
  • 🌿 Agarwood biosynthesis and fungal induction strategies
  • 💻 Public health genomics and bioinformatics pipelines

Ongoing Projects

  • Development of biotic/abiotic inducers for agarwood resin synthesis
  • Pan-genomic comparison of Pseudomonas aeruginosa
  • Metagenomic profiling of environmental & clinical microbiomes
