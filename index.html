About Me

Hello! Welcome to my homepage.

I am Kallyan Chakma, currently working as a Research Fellow at the Bangladesh Forest Research Institute. I specialize in microbial genomics, metagenomics, and the molecular biology of plant-microbe interactions.

I completed my undergraduate studies in Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology from the University of Chittagong.

My research focuses on optimizing fungal inoculation techniques for enhanced agarwood resin production — bridging traditional practices with modern molecular approaches.

Research Interests

  • Microbial and comparative genomics
  • Metagenomic analysis and antimicrobial resistance (AMR) profiling
  • Agarwood biosynthesis and fungal induction strategies
  • Public health genomics and bioinformatics workflows

Ongoing Projects

  • Development of biotic/abiotic inducers for agarwood resin synthesis
  • Pan-genomic comparison and AMR analysis of Pseudomonas aeruginosa
  • Metagenomic profiling of environmental and clinical microbiomes
