About Me

Hello! I am Kallyan Chakma, a Research Fellow at the Bangladesh Forest Research Institute. My focus is on microbial genomics, metagenomics, and bioinformatics. I hold a degree in Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology from the University of Chittagong. My research bridges traditional practices and modern molecular biology, especially in agarwood resin production.

Projects

  • Development of biotic/abiotic inducers for agarwood resin synthesis
  • Pan-genomic comparison and AMR analysis of Pseudomonas aeruginosa
  • Metagenomic profiling of environmental and clinical microbiomes

Publications

  • Chakma, K. et al. (2023). Agarwood biosynthesis: molecular insights and applied strategies. Journal of Plant Science.
  • Chakma, K. et al. (2022). Metagenomic analysis of forest soils. Microbial Ecology.
