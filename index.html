About Me

Hello! Welcome to my homepage.

I am Kallyan Chakma, currently working as a Research Fellow at the Bangladesh Forest Research Institute. I specialize in microbial genomics, metagenomics, and the molecular biology of plant-microbe interactions.

I completed my undergraduate studies in Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology from the University of Chittagong. I have worked extensively on pathogen genomics, antimicrobial resistance profiling, and viral genome surveillance, including dengue virus, WSSV, SARS-CoV-2, and drug-resistant *Klebsiella pneumoniae*.

At present, my research focuses on optimizing fungal inoculation techniques for enhanced agarwood resin production — an interface between traditional practices and modern molecular approaches.

Research Interests

  • Microbial and comparative genomics
  • Metagenomic analysis and antimicrobial resistance (AMR) profiling
  • Agarwood biosynthesis and fungal induction strategies
  • Public health genomics and bioinformatics workflows
